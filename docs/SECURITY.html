Security Policy

Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us at security@babelbridge.com. We will respond as quickly as possible to address the issue.

Acknowledgments

We appreciate the efforts of security researchers and are happy to acknowledge their contributions on our Hall of Fame page. See it at https://www.BabelBridge.com/Hall-of-Fame-Security

Preferred Languages

We accept vulnerability reports in English (en), Dutch (nl), and Japanese (jp).
